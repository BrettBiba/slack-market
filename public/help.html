Help

Invite @market to a channel. Mention a stock ticker and its price will appear in Slack.

The bot will try to respond to anything UPPERCASE longer than 2 characters or anything $UPPERCASE, prefixed with a dollar sign.

You can set dollars on to always require a dollar sign if you think the bot is too spammy.

You can set charts off to prevent charts from being displayed.

Buying and Selling

You can record buys with @market bought [symbol] and record sales with @market sold [symbol].
Approximate price is automatically recorded usng the last market price.
To see all positions use @market positions. To see someone else's positions use @market positions @user.
